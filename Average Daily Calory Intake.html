Average Daily Calory Intake

The average daily calorie intake depends on various factors such as age, weight, height, activity level, and health goals.
According to the Dietary Guidelines for Americans, the general range of calories for adults is 1,600 to 3,000 per day for men and 1,600 to 2,400 per day for women.
However, this may vary depending on the individual's needs and lifestyle.
Women over 50 may need fewer calories than younger women.
